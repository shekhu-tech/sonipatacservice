Refund & Cancellation Policy

Cancellation

Cancellations must be requested within 14 days of order. Orders already shipped or dispatched are not eligible for cancellation.

Returns

Items must be unused, in original packaging, and returned within 7 days to be eligible. Sale items may not be returnable.

Refund Process

Refunds, if approved, will be processed within 7 business days to your original payment method.

Exceptions

  • No refund/exchange on perishable items or services already rendered.
  • Damaged/defective items must be reported within 14 days of receipt.
  • Items under manufacturer warranty should be addressed to the manufacturer.

For any queries or to initiate a refund, please contact our support team.
